# My understanding of DevOps & Cloud Engineering
- DevOps is a culture or mindset where development team collaborate with operation team to reduce time to market by adopting automation, cloud, infrastructure & scaling.

- Cloud engineering is responsible for scaling and on-demand infrastructure creation, allowing team to build, deploy & manage apps without upfront cost.

Together, they help to achieve reliability, HA, faster releases, and efficient resource usage.
