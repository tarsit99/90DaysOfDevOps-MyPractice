# My understanding of DevOps & Cloud Engineering
- DevOps is a culture or mindset where development team collaborate with operation team to reduce time to market by implementing automation, cloud, infrastructure & scaling.

- Cloud engineering responsible for scaling and on-demand infrastructure creation, allowing team to build, deploy & manage apps without upfront cost.

Together, Devops & cloud engineers helps an org to achieve HA, reliability, faster releases and better resource utilization.
